The Grayson School National English Honor Society

What We Do

Society Details:

National english honor society is a group for students passionate about english literature. Our members are competent writers seeking to hone their craft and discuss current events in literature.

What:

Ready to find your voice?

This is a safe place to hone your skills with like-minded peers.

Who:

Grayson High School Students

Everyone, in any grade, is welcome to join. Only 10th and above are eligible to be members.

When:

Thursday, 12:00-12:45pm

Just 45 minutes every other week.

Where:

Room 211

We meet in Mr. King’s room. He’s our teacher sponsor.
